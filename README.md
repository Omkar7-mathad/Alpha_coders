## Project Overview

Choosing the right career path after Class 10 or Class 12 is a major challenge for many students in India. A lack of reliable guidance, limited awareness about courses and colleges, and insufficient access to career counseling often lead to confusion, poor decision-making, and high dropout rates.

The One-Stop Personalized Career & Education Advisor is a digital platform designed to provide students and parents with personalized career guidance, course recommendations, and college discovery tools. By leveraging AI-driven insights, structured career pathways, and regional accessibility, the platform helps students make informed academic and professional decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Students and parents frequently face several challenges while planning their academic careers:

- Limited awareness of available career options  
- Confusion in choosing streams after Class 10 and Class 12  
- Lack of access to reliable career counseling  
- Migration to expensive private colleges due to insufficient information about government institutions  
- High dropout rates caused by poor academic alignment  

These problems are more significant in rural and semi-urban areas where access to guidance and internet connectivity may be limited.

---

## Proposed Solution

This platform works as a centralized career guidance system that helps students identify suitable educational and career paths based on their interests, aptitude, and academic goals.

The platform provides:

- AI-based aptitude tests for personalized career suggestions  
- Clear career roadmaps from courses to future professions  
- Nearby college finder with important admission information  
- Personalized notifications for exams, admissions, and scholarships  
- Parent-friendly guidance features  
- Regional language support for better accessibility  

---

## Key Features

### Smart Aptitude Assessment
An interactive quiz analyzes students’ interests and strengths to recommend suitable academic streams and career paths.

### Career Pathway Visualization
Structured flowcharts guide students from:
Aptitude → Courses → Colleges → Career Opportunities

### College Finder
Students can discover nearby colleges based on:
- Course availability
- Cut-off marks
- Facilities and infrastructure
- Scholarships and financial aid

### Personalized Notifications
Students receive updates and reminders related to:
- Admission deadlines
- Entrance examinations
- Scholarship opportunities

### Parent Guidance Mode
A simplified interface designed to help parents understand career paths and academic planning.

### Regional Language Support
The platform supports regional languages so students from different regions can easily access guidance.
